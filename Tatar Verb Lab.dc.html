Counting rule

After a number, the noun stays singular — no plural suffix.

ике китап · биш кеше · ун ел

not ике китаплар

Compounds & ordinals

Just chain the words: егерме бер (21), йөз илле (150). For ordinals, only the last word takes the suffix:

егерме беренче = 21st

How many?

ничә = how many (countable) · күпме = how much / many
